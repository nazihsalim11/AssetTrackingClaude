# Project Scope Document
## Software Asset Tracking System for NPS
### Project Title
**Enterprise Asset Tracking & AMC Management System**

## 1. Project Overview
- The purpose of this project is to design and develop a centralized Software Asset Tracking System for managing and monitoring all company assets across departments, locations, and employees within the IT organization.
- The system will help track:
- Office infrastructure assets
- IT hardware assets
- Asset allocation and movement
- AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) details
- Purchase invoices and payment tracking
- QR code / barcode asset labeling
- Asset lifecycle and maintenance history
- The platform will improve operational visibility, accountability, inventory control, audit readiness, and maintenance management.

## 2. Project Objectives
- The proposed solution aims to:
- Centralize all asset information in one platform
- Reduce manual asset tracking processes
- Enable real-time asset visibility
- Improve asset utilization and accountability
- Track AMC renewals and service schedules
- Monitor vendor invoices and payment status
- Simplify auditing and reporting
- Introduce QR/Barcode-based asset identification
